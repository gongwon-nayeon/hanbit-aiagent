Global Electric Vehicle Sales Report by Year

Summary

According to BloombergNEF, global sales of electric vehicles are projected to reach 22 million in 2025, marking a 25% increase from 2024. China dominates these sales, making up nearly two-thirds, followed by Europe at 17% and the US at 7%.

Data from Virta shows that over 10 million electric vehicles were sold in 2022, a 60% increase from 2021, with more than 26 million electric cars on roads worldwide.

EV Volumes reports that global electric vehicle sales were 17.8 million in 2024 and are expected to increase to 21.3 million in 2025.
